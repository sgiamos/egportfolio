The Salon Guy

Clutch, Success, and Pre-Game are hair products sold by The Salon Guy.

Over a three week period, we worked to come up a re-brand to a few of The Salon Guy products to fit the new direction we wanted to take with the brand.

My Role:

Product Designer

Tools Used:

Adobe Illustrator

Overview

Clutch, Success, and Pre-Game are redesigns of previous pastes we used to sell. They were reformulated with a new and improved formula. We were looking to update the current design of tins that were being sold. We had several variations of pastes and creams being sold and wanted to minimize and combine a few together. These pastes can be purchased at www.thesalonguy.com.

Ideation

Color schemes we wanted were rich, bold colors, that would be easily recognizable and easy to distinguish between the three different products. We wanted to stick with a similar layout like our previous designs were but create something with a sophisticated style.
