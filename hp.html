HP Case Study

Analyzed and redesigned the HP Smart App. User tested the HP Tango printer.

This was a case study for HP that we conducted in teams in our User Experience Class. Our professor previously worked for HP and was able to get the company to send our class their HP Tango printers that we would set up and use with their HP Smart App.

My Role:

Designer
User Research

Tools Used:

Adobe XD

Overview

Our task was to evaluate the HP Smart app and assess how it worked with their HP Tango printer. We were also asked to conduct an OOBE (out-of-box experiment) tests and upon initial set up, we began our research and using the app. We were tasked to test the current application and redesign it based off our collected data.

Research

Conducted research on existing HP products that were on the market, along with the HP Tango printer and looked into the similarities and differences between them. Sixty participants were then surveyed about their current printer situation and their habits. We then used the data that was collected from those participants to formulate more detailed questions to conduct recorded interviews with four participants.

Personas

User Testing

We conducted an OOBE test, where we observed and recorded seven participants' actions to set up the HP Tango printer. The takeaways from this experiment from the participants was that the design of the printer was clean with a simple interface. Some pain points were network connectivity issues with the printer and confusion with the LED indicators on the printer.
