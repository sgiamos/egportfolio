Coo·e Coordinator

Replace noisy conversations, tiresome scheduling requests, and calendar polls with effortless coordination.

Coo·e is a social coordination app, that allows you to easily plan activities with your friends or colleagues, through having more streamlined coordination conversations. By replacing noisy conversations, tiresome scheduling requests, and calendar polls with effortless coordination.

UX Designer/Researcher

Overview

I have been working for Coo·e as a User Experience Designer and Researcher for 2 years. I work alongside a team of designers to come up with ideas to help enhance the experience for users. 

I am tasked with creating and updating interface designs, some of which are currently implemented. Along with writing up specifications for developers to establish a direction for the developers to follow. I also conduct usability and quality assurance tests, working closely with developers and file in depth incident reports to ensure that our application meets our current specifications. 

I have conducted user interviews where I have gathered information to present to our team. Where I then discuss what could be improved with our application and find resolutions. We are currently working on improving the application and its functionality, as well as new features that we hope to implement and test with users soon.
